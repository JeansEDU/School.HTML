Rogue-Like Games

What is an Rogue-Like Game?

A Rogue-Like is a game built with mechanics around losing. These games are defined by their randomly generated runs and rewarding yet punishing difficulty curves. Mastering these games requires both mechanical skill and the ability to maximize the most out of a bad or hopeless attempt.

Examples of Rogue-Likes

  • Hades 1 & 2
  • Noita
  • Risk of Rain 1 & 2
  • Bad North
