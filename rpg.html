RPG (Roleplaying Game)

What is an RPG?

An RPG is a "roleplaying game" where you play the part of a character that grows over the course of a long story. RPGs come in many different forms and the simplicity of the genre allows complexity in the story.

Examples of RPGs

Type of RPG Description Example Games
MMO Massive-Multiplayer Online game.
Emphasis on a world populated by a lot of players,
often influencing the economy and story of their world.
  • World of Warcraft
  • EVE Online
  • New World
Single-Player RPG's Purely Single Player Experiences, often offline.
Explore the progression of the player character
as they grow in power over the course of the game.
These games are often very story driven and motivated.
  • The Elder Scrolls
  • The Legend of Zelda
  • Deltarune
